About Me

Thomas Claret discovered his passion for theatre in a workshop at l’EcoleNormale Supérieure de Paris. Following the workshop, he moved to Canada, and studied acting at the Vancouver Academy of Dramatic Arts. Back in France, he continued his training as a physical theatre performer and trained at the Jacques Lecoq International School of Theatre. Following his graduation, he is currently working with the French director Lisa Wurmser. Thomas Claret is the co-founder of Compagnie des Xylophages in and performed in five shows of the company. He is also a member of the Sitio Company, an emerging theatre company based in Paris led by François Couder. Thomas is also highly trained in the martial arts (ninjutsu, karate, boxing whatever)
